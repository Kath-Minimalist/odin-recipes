The Best Oil-Free Hummus

Description

This is a recipe taken from the Forks Over Knives website. It is oil-free, tasty and virtually non-fat.

You can find a store-bought variation of this no-cook recipe in almost any grocery store, but most of them are made with added oil and tahini, which pushes up the fat content.

By Rip Esselstyn

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons Bragg Liquid Aminos
  • 1/4 cup water or vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients into a thick paste.
